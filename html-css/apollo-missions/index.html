The Apollo Program

Exploring Humanity's Journey to the Moon

The Apollo missions were a series of human spaceflight missions conducted by NASA between 1967 and 1972. Their goal was to land humans on the Moon and return them safely to Earth.

Fun Facts

  • 17 Apollo missions were launched.
  • 6 missions successfully landed on the Moon.
  • The first Moon landing occurred in 1969.
  • The final Apollo mission was Apollo 17 in 1972.

Why the Apollo Program Matters

The Apollo program advanced science, engineering, and space exploration. It inspired generations of scientists, engineers, and explorers while proving that humanity could travel to another world.
